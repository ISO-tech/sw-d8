Welcome to the new SW

October 12, 2001 | Page 3

BEGINNING WITH this issue, Socialist Worker will appear every week--bringing you all the news and analysis that you've come to expect, only twice as often.

Regular readers will notice some differences in the look of this paper. It's shorter than before, with a cleaner type and layout style. We hope this makes SW a more accessible read.

For this first issue, we've produced a 16-page paper--in the coming weeks, we'll reduce our size to 12 pages.

We're excited about this step that SW is taking. But we need the help of all of our readers. We need reports from all of the struggles that take place--in workplaces, in communities, on campuses. We need contributions that take up the questions facing our struggles.

And we need your financial help. Send your check to: SW, P.O. Box 16085, Chicago, IL 60616.

Papers like Socialist Worker rely on their readers--what they experience, what they think and what they plan to do about it. And we're proud to present all this twice as often.
