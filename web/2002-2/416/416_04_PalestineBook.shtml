A must-read for Palestine activists

August 2, 2002 | Page 4

Dear Socialist Worker,

Haymarket Books' new title The Struggle for Palestine is a must-read. Its 14 contributors tackle issues that every activist should be familiar with: Zionism, Israeli apartheid and the Arab working class, to name just a few of them.

The clear Marxist analysis presented by contributors puts the conflict into perspective for readers. And it pulls no punches--denouncing U.S. imperialism and corrupt Arab governments at the same time.

I encourage all of Socialist Worker's readers to go online to www.haymarketbooks.org and check it out!

John Green, Davis, Calif.
